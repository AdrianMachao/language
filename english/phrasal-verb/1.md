# take
## take off	
（飞机）起飞；脱下（衣物）；突然成功	
"The plane will take off at 3 PM."（飞机下午3点起飞。）
## take up	
开始（爱好）；占据（时间/空间）	
"She took up painting last year."（她去年开始学绘画。）
## take down	
取下；记下；拆毁	
"He took down the notes quickly."（他快速记下笔记。）
## take over	
接管；占据	
"The company was taken over by a competitor."（公司被竞争对手收购。）


## take on	
承担（责任）；呈现（特征）
"He took on extra work this month."（他这个月承担了额外工作。）
## take in
理解；收留；欺骗	
"I couldn’t take in all the information."（我无法理解全部信息。）
## take out	
取出；带…外出；消灭	
"She took her dog out for a walk."（她带狗出去散步。）
## take back	
收回（话语）；退货	
"I take back what I said earlier."（我收回之前的话。）

## take seriously	
认真对待
"Don’t take his jokes seriously."（别把他的玩笑当真。）
## take for granted	
认为理所当然	
"She took his help for granted."（她把他的帮助视为理所当然。）
## take pride in
以…为傲	"He takes pride in his work."
（他为自己的工作感到骄傲。）
## take advantage of	
利用；占便宜	"They took advantage of the discount."（他们利用了折扣优惠。）
## take place	
发生
"The meeting takes place on Monday."（会议周一举行。）
## take a break
休息
"Let’s take a break after lunch."（午饭后我们休息一下吧。）
## take a chance
冒险一试
"He took a chance and invested in Bitcoin."（他冒险投资了比特币。）
## take it easy
放松
"Take it easy! Don’t stress."（放轻松！别压力太大。）

# put

"She put on her jacket and left."（她穿上外套离开了。）
## put away
收好；储存
"Put away your toys after playing."（玩完后收好玩具。）
## put down
放下；记下；贬低
"Put down the book gently."（轻轻放下书。）
## put back
放回原处；推迟
"Please put the keys back."（请把钥匙放回去。）
## put off
推迟；使反感	"They put off the meeting until Friday."（会议推迟到周五。）
## put up with
忍受
"I can’t put up with this noise."（我受不了这噪音。）
## put forward
提出（建议）
"He put forward a new plan."（他提出了新计划。）
## put through
接通电话；完成
"The secretary put me through to the manager."（秘书帮我转接给经理。）
## put out
熄灭；出版；惹恼
"Firefighters put out the fire."（消防员扑灭了大火。）
## put at ease
使放松	"Her smile put me at ease."（她的微笑让我放松。）
## put in a good word
为…说好话
"Can you put in a good word for me?"（能帮我说句好话吗？）
## put into practice
付诸实践
"We’ll put the theory into practice."（我们将理论付诸实践。）
## put an end to
终止
"The law put an end to pollution."（法律终止了污染。）
## put two and two together
综合推断
"She put two and two together and guessed the truth."（她综合线索猜出了真相。）


# make
## make up	
编造；化妆；组成
She made up a story to explain her absence.（她编了个故事解释缺席。）
## make out
辨认出；理解；填写（表格）	I can't make out his handwriting.（我认不出他的字迹。）
## make into
把...改造成
They made the old barn into a cozy home.（他们把谷仓改造成了舒适的家。）
## make from
用...制作（原材料发生化学变化）
Paper is made from wood pulp.（纸是用木浆制成的。）
## make of
用...制作（物理形态改变）
The table is made of oak wood.（这张桌子是橡木做的。）
## make sb do sth
强迫某人做某事
My boss made me work late.（老板逼我加班。）
## make sure
确保
Make sure to lock the door.（记得锁门。）
## make it
成功；准时到达
After years of hard work, he finally made it.（多年努力后，他终于成功了。）
## make sb happy/sad
使某人开心/难过	Her words made him very happy.（她的话让他很开心。）
## make up for
弥补
He worked overtime to make up for his mistake.（他加班弥补错误。）
## make do with	
凑合用
We'll have to make do with one car.（我们得凑合用一辆车。）
## make room for
为...腾出空间
Can you make room for one more person?（能再挤一个人吗？）
## make clean
打扫干净
She made the kitchen clean.（她把厨房打扫干净了。）
## make ready
准备好
The team made ready for the competition.（队伍为比赛做好了准备。）
## make public
公开
The report was made public yesterday.（报告昨天公开了。
## make sense
有道理
His explanation doesn't make sense.（他的解释说不通。）
## make a difference
有影响
Small actions can make a big difference.（小举动也能有大影响。）
## make peace
和解
The two countries finally made peace.（两国终于和解了。）
## make a decision
做决定
It's time to make a decision.（该做决定了。）
## make money
赚钱
He makes money by investing.（他靠投资赚钱。）

# get

## get in

## get out

## get off

## get across

## get through
接通电话；完成
I finally got through to customer service.（我终于打通了客服电话。）
## get along (with)	
与...相处融洽

## get up

## get over

## get better/worse

## get started

## get going

## get rid of

## get ready
准备好
Get ready—we’re leaving in 5 minutes.（准备好，我们5分钟后出发。）
## get tired
感到疲倦
She got tired after working all day.（工作一整天后她累了。）
## get a chance
得到机会
I hope to get a chance to visit Japan.（我希望有机会去日本。）
## get lost
迷路
We got lost in the forest.（我们在森林里迷路了。）
## get married
结婚
They got married last summer.（他们去年夏天结婚了。）
