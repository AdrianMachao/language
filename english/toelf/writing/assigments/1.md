I believe that students should take a gap year after high school. One reason is that a gap year can help them figure out what career they want to pursue after graduating from university—a decision that has a great impact on their entire lives.
During a gap year, students have the opportunity to explore different fields and gain a better understanding of them, which helps clarify their career goals. For instance, a student who initially wanted to become a mathematician might realize that physics is their true passion and decide to become a physicist.
In addition, a gap year enables students to pursue self-discovery and personal growth, helping them mature and develop a deeper understanding of themselves and their interests. For example, during a gap year, they may travel to beautiful places and interact with people from various professions. These experiences expose students to captivating sights and diverse cultures, broaden their social knowledge, which leads to their increased maturity, self-awareness, and confidence.
