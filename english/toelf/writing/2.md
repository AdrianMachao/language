# Employee training
## Advantage
Employee turnover can be a problem to all companies. Providing trining to employee can lower the turnover rate since training programs can foster their sense of belongings.

Training employees is surely important, but it is not necessarily the most important consideration, There are numerous sectors that a company should invest in, public relations being a good example.

Frenquent training programs can interrupt the regularity of business operation. Both managers and employees would have to constantly stope what they are working on and strive to meet the training objectives.


## Disvantages
Ongoing train can beget unwanted results. For one thing, the self-esteem of those who are well-trained may become too high, Consequently, they may demand higher compensation than they deserve.

A determing factor of how far a company can go is definitly people. If the employee are not regualrly trained to keep up with developments in the market. the company will lose competitive edge.

whether trainings are effective or not, it serves as a platform where employees can interact with each other. They can take this opportunity to form strong ties, which is conducive to the morale of the whole company.
